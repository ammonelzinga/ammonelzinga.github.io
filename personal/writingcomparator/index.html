This website is an ongoing project to organize sacred texts, compare and contrast texts by asking questions and having AI determine a correct SQL selection statement, and then summarize the results. Uses AI (OpenAI API), Postgres (via Supabase), and embeddings for semantic search and comparison.

  • Organize and upload sacred texts and documents
  • Ask questions to compare and contrast texts
  • AI generates SQL selection statements to retrieve relevant passages
  • Summarizes results using AI
  • Built with Supabase, OpenAI API, and embeddings
